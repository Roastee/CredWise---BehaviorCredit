## THE PROBLEM — ₹4.2 Trillion Opportunity Ignored

**190 million Indians** are locked out of formal credit — not because they're unreliable, but because CIBIL can't see them.

| They exist | CIBIL sees |
|-----------|-----------|
| 18 months of UPI payments | Nothing |
| 4 years in an SHG | Nothing |
| 8 years of electricity bills on time | Nothing |
| Gig platform with 4.6★ rating | Nothing |

**Result:** 3.8Cr MSME loan applications rejected annually. ₹4.2T in credit demand unmet.

---

## THE SOLUTION — BehaviorScore™

> *"Your character should be your credit score."*

BehaviorCredit analyzes **8 alternative behavioral signals** to generate a **BehaviorScore (300–900)** in **under 30 seconds** — **without a bank account or CIBIL history**.

---

## 8-SIGNAL AI MODEL

| Signal | Weight | What We Measure |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| UPI Payment History | 25% | Frequency, diversity, consistency |
| Utility Bill Payments | 18% | Electricity, water streaks |
| SHG Participation | 15% | Savings discipline, attendance |
| Gig Platform Tenure | 10% | Rating, completion rate |
| E-commerce EMI | 8% | BNPL repayment behavior |
| Rental Proxy | 8% | UPI rent payments |
| App Usage | 8% | Banking app engagement |
| Recharge Patterns | 8% | Mobile data regularity |
